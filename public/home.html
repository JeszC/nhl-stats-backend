/home

Endpoints for the home page content. Top ten teams actually returns the full standings; this is by design so that the results can be filtered by conference or division into top ten parts.

Endpoints:

  • /getUpcomingGames/{date}
    • date: YYYY-MM-DD
  • /getTopTen/skaters
  • /getTopTen/goalies
  • /getTopTen/teams
  • /getLatestSeason
  • /getLatestUpcomingSeason
