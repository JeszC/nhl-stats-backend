/awards

Endpoints for award-related data. You can find the trophy category IDs and trophy IDs from the data supplied by the getTrophies-endpoint.

Endpoints:

  • /getTrophies
  • /getTrophyWinners/{trophyCategoryID}/{trophyID}
    • trophyCategoryID: number indicating the category to which the trophy belongs to (from 1 to 5 at least). These represent team, player, staff, etc.
    • trophyID: number indicating the trophy ID.
